Orca Whale



Orcas are apex predators, at the top of the food chain. No other animals (except for humans) hunt orcas.

  • Scientific Name: Orcinus orca
  • Average Length: 16-26ft
  • Average Lifespan: 50-80 years in the wild
  • Habitat: Orcas are found in the open ocean, but they seem to be most abundant in coastal waters.

The orca (killer whale) is a toothed whale and is the largest member of the Dolphin family.
